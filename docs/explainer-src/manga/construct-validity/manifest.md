## Concepts in dependency order

1. We trust the benchmark number — especially for "safety."
2. **Construct validity** — does the ruler actually touch the thing it claims to measure?
3. The systematic review — 29 reviewers, 445 benchmarks, three places measurement breaks.
4. The hidden numbers — exact-match scoring, no uncertainty, contested definitions, contamination.
5. The eight recommendations — how to build a benchmark that measures what matters.
6. The payoff — an honest score is smaller, and finally means something.
